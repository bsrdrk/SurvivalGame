##Places:
</br>
  * Safe House
    - Feature : Healing 
  * Cave
    - Enemy : Zombie (1 or 3)
    - Feature : Fight and Award
    - Stuff : Food
  * Forest
    - Enemy : Vampire (1 or 3)
    - Feature : Fight and Award
    - Stuff : Firewood
  * River
    - Enemy : Bear (1 or 3)
    - Feature : Fight and Award
    - Stuff : Water
  * Shop
   

You must have collected Water, Firewood and Food to finish the game.
